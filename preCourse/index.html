We don't stay down!
Stumble, crawl, take a minute, then get back up and finish what you started. We all trip, not all of us recover. Sometimes life seems determined to keep you down in the muck, don't let it.
People, and yes that means you, are incredibly resilient. We can bounce back from almost anything. Sure, there will be times when you can't even pull yourself up, that's when you crawl. Crawl your sorry butt to the nearest source of help and then take their hand, stand up, and get on with it.
An object at rest stays at rest, an object in motion stays in motion. If you stop at that first stumble and don't MOVE then there you'll stay. Direction isn't nearly as important as motion. Keep moving even if you don't quite know where you're going. The signposts will appear soon enough and you can alter your course as needed, but you'll never see those signs if you're face down in the dirt.

Falling down doesn't mean staying down!
